Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 26, 2026

1. Introduction and Data Safety

At BgRemover AI, we prioritize your security and operational privacy. Our primary service—removing backgrounds from digital images—is engineered to process entirely locally within your device's web browser framework using WebAssembly technology.

2. No Image Storage or Uploads

We do not collect, upload, stream, parse, or store the image files you submit to the tool. The Machine Learning inference occurs locally on your hardware. Because your files never traverse our servers, they are immune to cloud vulnerabilities and server-side data extraction.

3. Third-Party Advertising and Cookies

To keep the service free for our global user base, we monetize the platform through Google AdSense. Third party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this website or other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to users based on their visit to our sites and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

4. Log Files and Analytics

Like standard web applications, our hosting providers securely gather standard non-personally identifiable information automatically (e.g., browser type, ISP, referring/exit pages, date/time stamps). This data analyzes trends and administers site reliability.
